What is SmartCare HMS?

It is a hospital management platform built to organize patients, doctors, appointments, hospital workflows, and administration digitally.

Is the system role-based?

Yes. Patients, doctors, receptionists, and administrators each have their own dedicated workflows and access areas.

Can it be presented to clients?

Absolutely. The interface is designed to feel modern, clean, and professional enough for presentation and demonstration.
